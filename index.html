My Top Five Android Applications


Nova Launcher

Developer Description:

Nova Launcher replaces your home screen with one you control and can customize. Change icons, layouts, animations and more.

Why I like it:

Nova Launcher has been one of my favorite Android applications for awhile now. I like it because of how grainular it lets you get with your customizations. I recently found out that you can now add Google Now to the launcher by installing Nova Google Companion


Textra SMS

Developer Description:

A beautiful, super fast and highly customizable alternative to your stock Android messaging app.

Why I like it:

When I first started using Textra the stock Android app was pretty ugly and didn't have very many options. Textra on the other hand is an attractive SMS application that lets you customize just about anything.


Files by Google

Developer Description:

Files by Google is a file management app that helps you:

  • Free up space with cleaning recommendations
  • Find files faster with search and simple browsing
  • Share files offline with others, fast and without data
  • Back up files to the cloud to save you space on device

Why I like it:

Files by Google is not only a great file explorer, in my opinion the best part is how easy it makes freeing up extra space on your device.


KWGT Kustom Widget Maker

Developer Description:

With Kustom Widget you can create customized watches like Digital and Analog Clocks, Live Map Widget, Weather Widget, Text Widget, sophisticated Battery or Memory meters, randomly changing images, Music Players, World clocks, Astronomy widgets and much much more. Imagination is the limit.

Why I like it:

I just recently started using KWGT. I was having a hard time finding a home screen widget that had all the features I wanted and looked how I wanted. That's when I ran across KWGT Kustom Widget Maker and decided to just make my own.


Terminus

Developer Description:

Termius is the best way to manage, UNIX and Linux systems, whether that would be a local machine, a remote service, Docker Container, VM, Raspberry Pi, or AWS instance. In other words, Termius is Putty for Android but with awesome design.

Why I like it:

I really like Terminus. I use it to SSH into my Raspberry Pi to make changes and keep it up to date when I am not near a computer.
